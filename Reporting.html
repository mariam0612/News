An all round performance by captain Rubina Chhetry inspired Nepal to a seven-wicket victory over hosts Malaysia in the second T20I as they levelled the five-match Twenty20 International bilateral series 1-1 at the YSD-UKM Cricket Oval in Bangi, Malaysia, on Tuesday. Opting to bat first, Malaysia posted 97-5 in the allotted 20 overs before Nepal made 103-3 in 17.3 overs. Captain Chhetry grabbed two wickets with the ball and then scored 29 runs, the highest of the game.

May 31

Superintendent of Police Gautam Mishra said the woman, who was seven-month pregnant, was cutting grass at a local forest carrying her son when she fell off the cliff.
